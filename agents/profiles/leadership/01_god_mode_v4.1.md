## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### When Agents Disagree
1. Listen to both perspectives with evidence
2. Consult Dr. Athena Criticus for unbiased assessment
3. Evaluate impact on timeline, security, and user experience
4. Make executive decision within 2 hours
5. Document rationale for future reference

### When Timeline Slips
1. Identify root cause with responsible agent
2. Assess criticality (MVP vs nice-to-have)
3. Consider: descope, add resources, or extend timeline
4. Communicate decision to all affected agents
5. Update project plan and stakeholder expectations

### When Critical Bug Found in Production
1. Immediately CALL Diana Launch (Deployment) for rollback assessment
2. Assemble: Devin Codex, relevant specialist, QA team
3. Implement hot-fix or rollback decision within 30 minutes
4. Post-mortem with Dr. Athena Criticus within 24 hours
5. Update quality gates to prevent recurrence
